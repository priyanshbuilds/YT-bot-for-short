# Visual-skin library (rotate — 6 genuinely different looks, all hand-coded SVG / house quality)

The producer picks ONE skin per video that wasn't used in the last ~4 videos (`variation_ledger.md`).
Every skin is still bespoke code-drawn Remotion (NO stock/slideshow — the anti-template point), but the
palette, hero family, layout, motion signature, and caption style differ enough that consecutive videos
don't look mass-produced. Keep captions readable (bottom/upper third, sound-off legible) + one payoff button.

| id | Skin | Background | Accent + palette | Hero family | Layout | Motion signature | Captions |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| S1 | **Cinematic Gold** (original) | dark radial (`#0b1220`) | gold `#FFD23F` + 1 key + 3 topic | gauges/dials, cards, meters (ScoreDial/UtilBar/CardIcon) | center-stage single hero | springs, counters roll, path draw-on | 3-word karaoke, gold active word, bottom 170 |
| S2 | **Blueprint** | deep navy (`#0a1a2f`) grid | cyan `#39D2FF` + white line-art | schematic line-drawings, exploded diagrams, labeled parts | technical diagram, callout lines | stroke draw-on, blueprint "reveal" wipes | mono/technical font, cyan keyword, boxed |
| S3 | **Bright Pop** | cream/light (`#FDF6E3`) | bold primary red/blue/yellow | chunky flat-illustration objects, thick black outlines | big playful shapes, off-center | bouncy overshoot, squash-stretch | Anton/heavy black, black-outline, keyword in red |
| S4 | **Data Room** | near-black (`#07090d`) | neon green `#36E07A` + red `#FF5A4D` | charts, bars, tickers, big numbers, gauges | dashboard / split panels | counters, bar-fills, ticker scroll, glow | terminal/mono, green numbers, tight |
| S5 | **Paper Cutout** | warm paper texture (`#efe7d6`) | marker primaries | hand-drawn/cutout props (à la Gohar Khan 19.5M) — drawn cards, sheets, signs | collage, props enter/exit fast | snappy hard cuts, paper "flip"/drop, ~1 prop per phrase | marker/handwritten, ALL-CAPS upper third |
| S6 | **Neon Night** | black + subtle vignette | magenta `#FF4FD8` + cyan glow | glassmorphic panels, glowing outlines, orbs | floating glass cards, depth | pulse/glow, parallax, soft float | thin bright font, neon keyword, glow shadow |

**Rules:** don't reuse a skin within 4 videos; match the skin to the format where noted in `formats.md`
(e.g. Data-Room for case-study/number, Paper-Cutout for story, Blueprint for how-it-works). Keep the
**phrase-locked visual change** learned-rule in EVERY skin (transform the hero on each phrase). The skin
sets palette/hero/motion — the topic still drives the specific visuals. S5 (Paper Cutout) directly applies
the Gohar Khan technique from `competitor_playbook.md`.
