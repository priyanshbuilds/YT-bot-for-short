Your hair stands on end, the air starts to tingle, and you realize you're about to be struck by lightning.
There's no time to reach shelter. So do not lie flat, because the ground itself carries the current sideways through your body.
Instead, crouch low on the balls of your feet with your heels touching and your hands over your ears.
This makes you a small target and gives the current less of you to flow through. And never hide under a lone tall tree.
The grim truth? Lightning is five times hotter than the sun, so a near miss can still stop your heart.
