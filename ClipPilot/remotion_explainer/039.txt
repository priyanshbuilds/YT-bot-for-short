Did you know there is one organ in your body that can completely regrow itself?
It's your liver.
You see, if someone donates up to 70% of their liver,
The remaining piece will start to rapidly divide and multiply.
Within just a few weeks, both the donor's and the recipient's liver will grow back to full size.
It's the only internal organ we have with this incredible superpower,
And it's why living liver donations are even possible.
