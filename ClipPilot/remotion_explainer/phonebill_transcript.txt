What if half the taxes on your phone bill aren't actually taxes? Scroll past the plan, under fees and surcharges, you'll see lines like Regulatory Recovery, Administrative Charge, or Network Cost Recovery. Real taxes are set by the government, but those lookalike fees are set by your carrier, and many add five to fifteen dollars a month. On a sixty-five dollar plan, that quiet stack can push your real bill closer to eighty. Snap a recent bill into a free AI and ask which line items are government taxes, and which are carrier fees you can ask about. Stop paying for the disguise.
