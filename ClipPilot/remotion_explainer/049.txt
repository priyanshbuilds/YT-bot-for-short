You wake to a room full of smoke so thick you can't see your own hands. Don't stand up, because the air near the ceiling is a furnace of toxic gas that can knock you out in seconds. Instead, drop to the floor and crawl, where a thin layer of breathable air still hugs the ground. Now feel every door before you open it. If the handle is hot, fire is waiting on the other side, so find another way. The chilling fact? Smoke, not flames, kills most fire victims, often while they're still asleep and never even wake up.
