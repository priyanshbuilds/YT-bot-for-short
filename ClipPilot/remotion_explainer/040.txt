Did you know you actually have a second brain inside your body?
It's called the enteric nervous system, and it lives entirely in your gut.
This network is made up of over 100 million neurons,
Which is more than you have in your spinal cord.
It's so independent that even if the connection to your actual brain is severed,
Your gut will continue to function completely on its own.
So the next time you have a "gut feeling" about something,
It's literally your second brain trying to talk to you.
