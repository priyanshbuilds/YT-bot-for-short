Your engine dies right on the railroad crossing, and far down the line a train horn begins to wail. Forget the car, because a train can't stop in time and the impact will crush it like a can. So get out immediately and leave everything behind. Now here's the part nobody knows: don't run away from the tracks at a right angle. Run toward the oncoming train at a forty-five degree angle. That way, when it smashes your car, the flying debris hurtles forward, away from where you're sprinting. The terrifying truth? A speeding train needs over a mile to fully stop.
