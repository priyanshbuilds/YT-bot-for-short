Somewhere out there, light is screaming... and it can never escape. This is the most terrifying object in the universe. A black hole is born when a giant star runs out of fuel. Its core collapses under its own crushing weight, until gravity grows so violent that not even light can leave. The edge is called the event horizon. The point of no return. Cross it, and there is no coming back. Gravity pulls your feet harder than your head, stretching you into a thread of atoms. We can't see the hole itself, only the searing gas spiraling in at near light-speed. In 2019, humanity captured that fire for the first time. And at the center waits the singularity, where everything you ever were is crushed into nothing.
