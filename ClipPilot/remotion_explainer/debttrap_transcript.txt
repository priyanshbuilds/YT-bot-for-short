What if your minimum payment is designed to keep you in debt for decades? Banks set it tiny on purpose, about two percent of the balance, not a flat amount. On a one thousand dollar card at twenty two percent APR, the minimum drops just three dollars off principal a month. Pay only the minimum and that one thousand takes over twenty eight years to clear. Switch to a flat one hundred a month instead, and the same debt is gone in eleven. Stop paying the minimum.
