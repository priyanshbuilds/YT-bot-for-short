Do you ever cough up these small, white stones?
Well, they are actually called tonsil stones.
You see, your tonsils are filled with tiny pits and crevices.
And when you eat, small pieces of food can get trapped in these pockets.
Over time, bacteria feeds on this food, which creates a foul-smelling film.
This film then hardens or calcifies, turning into the little stones that you see.
They usually fall out on their own, but they're the main reason for bad breath.
