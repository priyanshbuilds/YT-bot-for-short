Have you ever wondered how caffeine actually keeps you awake?
You see, throughout the day, your brain produces a chemical called adenosine.
And this chemical binds to receptors in your brain, causing you to feel sleepy.
But caffeine has a very similar structure to adenosine,
Which allows it to bind to these exact same receptors.
This essentially blocks the adenosine from doing its job,
Which stops you from feeling tired.
